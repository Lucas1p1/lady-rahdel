About Me

A dependable extra pair of hands for healthcare teams

Over the last two months I've trained intensively as a Medical Virtual Assistant, building real fluency in patient-facing administration, clinical documentation, and the compliance standards that protect patient trust.

I'm Rahmat Adeola Dawood, a Medical Virtual Assistant supporting clinics and providers remotely. My focus is simple: handle the administrative and documentation load accurately and on time, with patient privacy treated as non-negotiable at every step.

I came into this field through structured, hands-on MVA training covering intake, EMR systems, medical coding, and HIPAA-compliant communication, and I've kept building on it every week since.

"Whether it's a scheduling calendar, an EMR entry, or a SOAP note, I hold myself to the same standard: accurate, fast, discreet."
